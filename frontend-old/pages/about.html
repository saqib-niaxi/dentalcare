About Dr. Ahmed Dental Care

Dedicated to providing exceptional dental care for over 15 years

Dr. Ahmed Khan, BDS, MDS

Dr. Ahmed Khan is a highly qualified and experienced dental surgeon with over 15 years of practice in providing comprehensive dental care. He completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from Karachi Medical and Dental College and later pursued his Masters in Dental Surgery (MDS) specializing in Prosthodontics.

His passion for dentistry and commitment to patient care has made him one of the most sought-after dental practitioners in Karachi. He believes in providing gentle, pain-free dental treatments using the latest techniques and technology.
